# Retrospective

## What Went Well
- The scope statement I created helped me stay within my scope and avoid any scope creep.
- The website I created has a good structure and consistent design throughout both pages.
- The project plan was well organized and included risk analysis along with a work breakdown structure. 

## What Went Wrong
- My lack of familiarity with GitHub caused several issues that took up excess time to resolve.
- The website is structured correctly, but without the pictures under the product, it suffers in quality.
- Some aspects of the project took longer than expected, resulting in time management issues.

## What I Would Do Differently
- Manage my time better to polish the website and make it higher quality.
- Add pictures to my product to further increase the website quality.
- Increase my familiarity with GitHub to be more comfortable and resolve issues more quickly.
