# **Project Plan**

**Project** : Enrich Apparel - Online Clothing Brand Launch
**Duration** : 16-18 weeks
**Budget** : $15,000
**Priority** : Time > Quality > Budget

# **Risk Analysis (TAME Framework)**

1. **Website Payment Processing Failure**

TAME Strategy - Transfer
The security burden is reduced by transferring the risk to a trusted third-party payment processor. 

2. **Low Initial Sales**

TAME Strategy - Mitigate
The likelihood of a weak launch performance is reduced by conducting early market research and building a presence on social media before the official launch. 

3. **Budget Overrun**

TAME Strategy - Accept
Since the budget is low priority, the risk will be accepted as long as the timeline and quality are protected. 

4. **Supplier Production Delays**

TAME Strategy - Mitigate
By having backup suppliers and placing shipment orders early, the risk of supplier delays can be mitigated.

5. **Product Quality Issues**

TAME Strategy - Mitigate
Probability of poor quality at launch can be mitigated by inspecting product samples and refining specifics before starting bulk production. 

6. **Scope Creep**

TAME Strategy - Eliminate
This risk can be removed by enforcing the boundaries set in the project scope statement. 

# **Work Breakdown Structure**

**Phase 1: Initiation (Weeks 1-3)**
- Review market research
- Identify intended demographic
- Define charter for project

**Phase 2: Branding & Planning (Weeks 4-6)**
- Brand identity approved
- Create logo
- Finalize color of product
- Develop brand mission

**Phase 3: Development of Product (Weeks 6-10)**
- Design 5 initial products
- Select a supplier
- Sample products
- Product design and quality approved

**Phase 4: Development of Website (Weeks 8-14)**
- Choose platform for e-commerce
- Create layout for website
- Integrate secure checkout
- Implement product pictures and descriptions
- Website functionality review

**Phase 5: Preperations for Marketing (Weeks 12-16)**
- Run advertising pre-launch
- Create calendar for content
- Create social media accounts

**Phase 6: Launch & Evaluation (Weeks 15-18)**
- Pre-launch review
- Public launch of website
- Monitor sales 
- Post-launch evaluation
