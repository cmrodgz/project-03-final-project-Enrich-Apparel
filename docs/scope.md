# Project Scope Statement

## Project Name
Enrich Apparel - Online Clothing Brand Launch

## Project Purpose
The purpose for the project is to develop, design, and launch Enrich Apparel, a small online clothing brand for adults ages 18-28. The result of this project is to have a fully branded store with an initial product line of five clothing items. 

## Why Are We Doing This Project?
This project is being done to simulate a realistic small-business scenario that demonstrates the principles of project management. There will be aspects of planning, scheduling, branding, product development, budgeting, and risk management involved. 

## Description
The brand Enrich Apparel will be clothing available online-only, and will include t-shirts, sweatpants, and hoodies.

The project will include:
- Market research
- Website creation
- Supplier selection
- Design for five initial products
- Official online launch
- Brand development
- Social media marketing

## Desired Results
For a successful result, the project must accomplish the following:
- Stay within the budget of $15,000
- Develop the five initial apparel items
- Launch the e-commerce website by Week 16
- Achieve a minimum of 60 sales within the first 30 days of launch
- Establish an active social media presence

## Time
The final deadline would be 18 weeks. 
The ideal projected deadline is 16 weeks. 

## Quality
Finished product means:
- Fully functional website with a secure checkout process
- Branding consistency throughout social media and website
- No major website issues or checkout errors
- All five initial products are purchasable
- All products meet material and print standards

Quality tests will be verified through sample product reviews and transaction testing before the launch deadline. 

## Budget
The total budget is $15,000.

## Estimated Allocation:
- Branding and Logo Design: $1,500
- Initial Inventory: $4,500
- Product Samples: $3,000
- Advertising and Marketing: $2,500
- Website Hosting and Development: $2,500
- Contingency Reserve: $1,000

## People Resources
Key team members:
- Project Manager - Oversees scheduling, planning, and execution of timeline
- Web Developer - Creates and tests e-commerce website
- Marketing Lead - Manages promotional campaigns and all social media
- Graphic Designer - Creates apparel designs and logo for branding
- Supplier - Produces the clothing items

## Feedback Loops
- Week 2/3 - Review market research
- Week 4 - Brand identity approved
- Week 8 - Product design approved
- Week 13 - Website functionality review
- Week 15 - Pre-launch review

Providers of feedback include: 
- Sponsors
- Team members
- Target customers

## Priorities
- Time: 1 (Most important)
- Quality: 2 (Second priority)
- Budget: 3 (Least important)

## Exclusions
- Creation of a physical storefront
- More than five initial products
- Mobile app development
- International shipping at launch
- Influencer contracts exceeding the budget
- Wholesale distribution agreements

## Acceptance Criteria
Project will be considered complete when:
- The website has launched and is publicly accessible
- Secure process for payment is functioning accordingly
- Total budget of $15,000 is not exceeded
- Five products are available to be purchases
- Branding material is finalized

Final approval must be provided by:
- Project Manager
- Project Sponsor
